Thankmas - December 2023

Jan 2, 2024

Throughout the month of December, 9 streamers joined together as a team, and raised $666.66 for Thankmas 2023 created by Jacksepticeye.

Over the past five years, Thankmas has raised over $22 Million for important causes. For the second year in a row, Thankmas is fundraising for World Central Kitchen (WCK), a nonprofit organization that is first to the frontlines providing fresh meals in response to crises around the world.

This was of course a team effort, led by DashGames47. Make sure to check out these amazing streamers.
