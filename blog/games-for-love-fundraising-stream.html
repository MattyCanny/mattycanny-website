Games For Love Fundraising Stream

Dec 15, 2023

At the beginning of December, I teamed up with an agency to raise funds for a charity which supports children all around the world by holding a charity stream event.

Throughout the stream I carried out different incentives such as dressing up, and eating spicy beans when we met different fundraising targets. Overall the community donated $750 to Games for Love which is an amazing total.

Games for Love is a 501(c)(3) Gaming Charity dedicated to easing suffering, saving lives, and creating sustainable futures for children.
